## Getting Started

AudioStreaming is an innovative learning platform that uses AI-powered voice conversations to help you learn new topics, practice languages, and engage in interactive educational experiences.

### What You'll Need
- A modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge)
- A microphone for voice input
- Speakers or headphones for audio output
- A stable internet connection
- A registered account

---

## Registration and Login

### Creating an Account

1. **Navigate to the Registration Page**
   - Open your browser and go to the AudioStreaming website
   - Click on "Register" or "Sign Up"

2. **Fill in Your Details**
   - **Name**: Enter your full name
   - **Email**: Provide a valid email address
   - **Password**: Create a strong password (minimum 6 characters)
   - **Confirm Password**: Re-enter your password

3. **Submit Registration**
   - Click the "Register" button
   - You'll be automatically logged in and redirected to the setup wizard

### Logging In

1. **Go to the Login Page**
   - Click "Login" on the homepage

2. **Enter Credentials**
   - Email address
   - Password

3. **Access Your Dashboard**
   - Click "Login" to access your personalized dashboard

### Password Requirements
- Minimum 6 characters
- Recommended: Mix of uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and special characters

## Setting Up Your AI Teacher

After registration, you'll be guided through the Setup Wizard to personalize your learning experience.

### Step 1: Choose Your AI Personality

Select from three AI teacher personalities:

**🌟 Friendly Teacher**
- Warm and encouraging
- Patient and supportive
- Great for beginners
- Uses positive reinforcement

**🎓 Strict Professor**
- Professional and formal
- Challenging and rigorous
- Ideal for advanced learners
- Focuses on accuracy and depth

**🎉 Fun Buddy**
- Casual and entertaining
- Uses humor and analogies
- Perfect for relaxed learning
- Makes learning enjoyable

### Step 2: Set Your Learning Preferences

**Language**
- Choose your preferred learning language
- Options: English, Spanish, French, German, Hindi, and more

**Proficiency Level**
- **Beginner**: New to the subject
- **Intermediate**: Some prior knowledge
- **Advanced**: Experienced learner

**Communication Style**
- **Formal**: Professional and structured
- **Casual**: Relaxed and conversational
- **Balanced**: Mix of both

**Response Length**
- **Concise**: Brief, to-the-point answers
- **Detailed**: Comprehensive explanations
- **Balanced**: Moderate detail level

### Step 3: Define Your Learning Goals

**Focus Areas**
- Select topics you want to learn (e.g., Programming, Languages, Science)
- Add custom topics specific to your interests

**Learning Goals**
- Set specific objectives (e.g., "Learn Python basics", "Improve Spanish conversation")
- Track progress toward these goals

**Session Preferences**
- **Session Duration**: 15, 30, 45, or 60 minutes
- **Frequency**: Daily, weekly, or custom schedule

### Step 4: Review and Confirm

- Review all your settings
- Click "Complete Setup" to save your preferences
- You can change these settings anytime from the Settings page

## Using Audio Learning

The Audio Learning feature is the core of AudioStreaming, enabling real-time voice conversations with your AI teacher.

### Starting a Learning Session

1. **Navigate to Audio Learning**
   - Click "Audio Learning" in the navigation menu
   - Or select "Start Learning" from your dashboard

2. **Grant Microphone Permissions**
   - Your browser will request microphone access
   - Click "Allow" to enable voice input

3. **Begin Conversation**
   - Click the "Start Session" button
   - The AI will greet you based on your chosen personality
   - Start speaking when you're ready

### During a Session

**Speaking to the AI**
- Speak clearly and at a normal pace
- The AI will process your speech in real-time
- Wait for the AI to finish responding before speaking again

**Listening to Responses**
- The AI responds with voice audio
- Responses are tailored to your proficiency level
- Text transcripts appear on screen for reference

**Session Controls**
- **Pause**: Temporarily pause the conversation
- **Resume**: Continue the conversation
- **End Session**: Finish and save the session

### Types of Interactions

**Ask Questions**
- "Can you explain quantum physics?"
- "How do I use async/await in JavaScript?"
- "What's the difference between ser and estar?"

**Practice Conversations**
- "Let's practice ordering food in French"
- "Can we discuss current events?"
- "Help me prepare for a job interview"

**Request Explanations**
- "Explain this concept in simpler terms"
- "Can you give me an example?"
- "What are the practical applications?"

**Get Feedback**
- "How am I doing?"
- "What should I focus on improving?"
- "Can you test my understanding?"

### Session Features

**Real-time Transcription**
- See your words and the AI's responses as text
- Helpful for reviewing conversations
- Useful for language learning

**Context Awareness**
- The AI remembers your conversation history
- Builds on previous topics discussed
- Maintains continuity across sessions

**Adaptive Difficulty**
- AI adjusts complexity based on your responses
- Gradually increases challenge as you improve
- Provides appropriate support when needed

### Ending a Session

1. **Click "End Session"**
   - Or say "Let's end the session"

2. **Session Summary**
   - Review topics covered
   - See time spent learning
   - View key takeaways

3. **Save and Archive**
   - Sessions are automatically saved
   - Access past conversations from your dashboard
   - Review transcripts anytime

## Participating in Discussions

Engage with the community and get AI-powered insights on various topics.

### Viewing Discussions

1. **Navigate to Discussions**
   - Click "Discussions" in the menu

2. **Browse Topics**
   - See all active discussions
   - Filter by category or popularity
   - Search for specific topics

3. **View Discussion Details**
   - Click on any discussion to view full details
   - Read posts from other users
   - See AI-generated responses

### Creating a Discussion

1. **Click "New Discussion"**

2. **Fill in Details**
   - **Title**: Clear, descriptive title
   - **Category**: Choose relevant category (Technology, Science, Language, etc.)
   - **Description**: Detailed description of the topic
   - **Tags**: Add relevant tags for discoverability

3. **Submit**
   - Click "Create Discussion"
   - Your discussion is now live

### Adding Posts

1. **Open a Discussion**
   - Click on the discussion you want to contribute to

2. **Write Your Post**
   - Click "Add Post"
   - Type your message or question
   - Format with markdown if desired

3. **Submit Post**
   - Click "Post" to add your contribution
   - Your post appears in the discussion thread

### Getting AI Responses

1. **Request AI Insight**
   - Click "Get AI Response" on any discussion
   - The AI analyzes the discussion context

2. **AI Analysis**
   - Provides expert insights
   - Offers different perspectives
   - Suggests resources or next steps

3. **Interact with AI Response**
   - Read the AI's contribution
   - Ask follow-up questions
   - Share insights with the community

### Discussion Best Practices

**Be Respectful**
- Treat all participants with respect
- Avoid offensive or inappropriate content
- Stay on topic

**Be Clear**
- Write clear, concise posts
- Use proper formatting
- Include relevant context

**Be Engaged**
- Respond to others' posts
- Share your knowledge

**Be Constructive**
- Provide helpful feedback

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues and Solutions

#### Audio Not Working

**Problem**: Can't hear AI responses
**Solutions**:
- Check speaker/headphone volume
- Verify browser audio permissions
- Refresh the page
- Try a different browser

**Problem**: AI can't hear you
**Solutions**:
- Check microphone permissions
- Test microphone in system settings
- Ensure microphone is not muted
- Try a different microphone

#### Connection Issues

**Problem**: Session disconnects frequently
**Solutions**:
- Check internet connection
- Close unnecessary browser tabs
- Disable VPN temporarily
- Try wired connection instead of Wi-Fi

**Problem**: Slow response times
**Solutions**:
- Check internet speed
- Clear browser cache
- Close other applications
- Try during off-peak hours

#### Login Problems

**Problem**: Can't log in
**Solutions**:
- Verify email and password
- Check for typos
- Reset password if forgotten
- Clear browser cookies

**Problem**: Session expires quickly
**Solutions**:
- Check "Remember Me" option
- Verify browser cookie settings
- Log out and log back in

#### Display Issues

**Problem**: Page not loading correctly
**Solutions**:
- Refresh the page (Ctrl+R or Cmd+R)
- Clear browser cache
- Update browser to latest version
- Try incognito/private mode

**Problem**: Text not displaying
**Solutions**:
- Check browser zoom level
- Disable browser extensions
- Try different browser
